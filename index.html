How this kit differs from standard CDC / FEMA guidelines

Removed heavy water/food stockpiles — dorm access to campus dining limits storage feasibility
Added high-filtration masks and rapid tests — not included in general FEMA emergency kits
Added mental health section — isolation in dorm housing creates distinct psychological risk
All items compact and portable — fits in a single backpack for a mobile, high-density living context
